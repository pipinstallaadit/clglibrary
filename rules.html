Library

This is the resource for Students / Faculty / Staff  this is where you first come for information and research.

Quiet place to sit:

The library is one of the favorite quiet places to sit / study and read.

Food, drinks:

First things first. The canteen / cafeteria is on the ground floor near the quadrangle.

Timings:

9.00 am to 5.30 pm (Pl. check for exact time) Offers tea / coffee, snacks, sandwiches, Lunch etc. (At a cost)

Cell phones:

Cell phones today are a critical piece of staying in touch any where any time. Use be restricted to academic purposes or emergency . Either turn them off or set them to silent mode. Take any conversations — phone or in person — outside the Library. (Stand on the right, walk on the left and make everybody happy) (Library is manned on Self-Discipline and Trust)
