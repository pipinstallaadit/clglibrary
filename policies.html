BOOK BANK BOOKS

Your previous records if any have to be clear. In the beginning of the academic year, books are loaned to you for the full year, that is both the semesters. Look out for the detailed notices on the VJTI site / Library Notice Board ( July / August )

REPROGRAPHY

Is given of what is required, and what is in the Library, in keeping with the copy right laws, and the possibility of Photocopying the required material. The charge is 50p. per page of photocopy. Payment is to be made in the accounts section before 2.00 P.M The Photocopy material will be given to you as soon as it is ready depending on the load at the time. You must come and ask for it after allowing at least four hours, to get the work done. Preferably come on the following day.

RESERVES / CLAIMS

If you find that a book is not available, you can place a CLAIM. Claimed Books are issued only after 3 P.M and the fine is more. You have to enquire and see whether your demand has materialized, preferably between 3 P.M to 4.30 P.M. The book will be kept for you for two days after which . your claim is forfeited.

LOST / DAMAGED

In case you lose or damage a book, please report at the counter immediately. Take the instructions for replacement of the book from the Librarian. Check the condition of all the material / s before issue, you will be held responsible for damage. Library resources must be handled with care. As a responsible member, please help us to maintain the library material in good condition. All library material must first be issued from the issue counter before it is used. Please collect your ID Card before leaving the Library. Library material issued for current reading against the ID Card, is not to be taken out of the Library.
